Access to data

A data repository is currently being developed and will be available in the near future. For anyone who requests access to our data, we like to stipulate the following:

  1. Please describe how the data will be used and how they may help your studies.
  2. Please cite the paper below in your publications to which our data have contributed.

    Citation: Cao, J.; Song, H.K.; Yang, H.; Castillo, V.; Chen, J.; Clendenin, C.; Rosen, M.; Zhou, R.; Pickup, S. Respiratory Motion Mitigation and Repeatability of Two Diffusion-Weighted MRI Methods Applied for Orthotopic Pancreatic Cancer in Mice. 2021, 7, x. https://doi.org/10.3390/xxxxx
